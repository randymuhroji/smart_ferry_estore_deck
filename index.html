01 — Executive Summary

The Opportunity Hiding in Plain Sight

MV Putri Anggraini currently operates the Batam–Johor corridor — one of the busiest sea crossings in Southeast Asia. With the upcoming launch of Batam–Singapore and Singapore–Johor routes, the vessel is positioned to become the premier tri-corridor ferry operator in the region.

Yet today, most tickets are sold at counters or via apps. The physical walk-in audience — untapped, impulsive, high-intent — remains completely uncaptured. The Smart Ferry E-Store changes that.

The E-Store is a premium interactive kiosk concept designed to convert foot traffic into booked passengers — placed in terminals, malls, hotels, and tourist zones across all three ports.

Where Today's Channels Fall Short

Every existing sales channel leaves value on the table. The E-Store closes every gap.

📱

App & Web Dependency

Digital channels only serve travelers who already intend to buy. The spontaneous, walk-in audience is missed entirely — a massive untapped pool.

⏱️

Counter Congestion

Traditional counters create long queues, staffing costs, and a friction-heavy experience that drives frustration rather than loyalty.

💡

Zero Impulse Capture

🎨

Lack of Premium Experience

Existing purchase channels offer no brand differentiation. The booking experience is purely transactional — a missed opportunity to build loyalty.

Tourist Unfriendliness

14 — Implementation Roadmap

From Concept to Pilot in 6 Months

A structured five-phase rollout designed to minimize risk and maximize learnings from day one.

🔍
Phase 01 · Month 1–2
Discovery & Design

Stakeholder alignment, site surveys across Batam Centre, Stulang Laut, and HarbourFront. UX/UI design, hardware specification, brand system development, and vendor engagement. Pilot locations confirmed.

🔧
Phase 02 · Month 2–4
Prototype Development

Physical kiosk manufacturing, software development, payment integration across SGD/IDR/MYR, gamification build, and internal testing with simulated booking scenarios for all three routes.

🚀
Phase 03 · Month 4–5
Pilot Deployment

Install 3–5 units at Tier 1 locations — Batam Centre terminal, VivoCity Singapore, and Stulang Laut terminal. Staff training completed. Soft launch with real-time monitoring dashboard activated.

📊
Phase 04 · Month 5–6
Optimization Sprint

Data analysis from pilot units. UX iteration, conversion optimization, gamification tuning, and promotional strategy refinement based on real passenger behavior across all routes.

🌐
Phase 05 · Month 7+
Network Expansion

Scale to 15–30 units across Singapore, Batam, and Johor. New route kiosk content activated as Batam–SG and SG–Johor routes launch. Regional loyalty ecosystem fully operational.
