09 — Gamification & Engagement

Every Booking Memorable & Rewarding

Gamification transforms a transactional moment into a delightful experience — increasing conversion, dwell time, and repeat purchasing.

Instant Reward

Spin & Sail Reward

Every booking triggers a spin wheel moment — from percentage discounts to free lounge access. Creates excitement and urgency to buy.

Urgency Driver

Lucky Flash Voucher

Time-limited surprise discounts appear at certain hours, creating a "catch it before it's gone" impulse that drives immediate decisions.

Loyalty Builder

Travel Mission Board

Passengers collect stamps across routes — complete missions like "Visit all 3 ports" to unlock exclusive travel rewards and status upgrades.

Repeat Purchase

Sail Points System

Every purchase earns redeemable Sail Points. Accumulated points unlock discounts, free tickets, lounge access, or partner perks.

Premium Upsell

Executive Tier Unlocks

Frequent users progress through Gold, Platinum, and Diamond tiers — unlocking lounge access, priority boarding, and concierge perks.

Ecosystem Play

Partner Reward Bundles

Gamification integrates with hotel, F&B, and attraction partners. A ferry booking triggers a partner offer — seamless regional loyalty.
